Definition of Streaming


---------------

A multimedia file that can be played instantly without having to be downloaded first is referred to as streaming. If a piece of media, whether recorded or live, can be viewed online and played on any device without being downloaded, it is considered to be streamed media. It operates by sending media content continuously from a server to the client.
A live stream is a broadcast of an event that takes place online. The most popular live streaming events are award shows, sporting events, boxing contests, video games, and unique one-time events.

Characteristics of a good stream


----------------------

The stream's content must be for the target audience in order for the host to entertain and engage its viewers. If the right content is present, the video stream will be successful because the target viewers will support it.

A good stream must be lag-free and have good audio and high-definition video meaning it won’t pixelate.

For a peaceful stream and to avoid demonetization a good video stream needs quality control such as a team that can remove offensive comments
